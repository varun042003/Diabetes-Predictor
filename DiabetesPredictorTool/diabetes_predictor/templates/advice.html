  • Maintain a balanced diet with plenty of fruits and vegetables

  • Include lean proteins in your meals
  • Limit processed foods and added sugars
  • Stay hydrated with water instead of sugary drinks

Physical Activity

  • Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate activity daily
  • Include both cardio and strength training exercises
  • Find activities you enjoy to stay motivated
  • Break up periods of sitting with short activity breaks
  • Consider tracking your steps with a fitness app

  • Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night
  • Establish a regular sleep schedule
  • Practice stress-reduction techniques like meditation
  • Take time for activities you enjoy
  • Stay connected with supportive friends and family
